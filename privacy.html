Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 29 May 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Blockchain Advisors Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects the personal information of users ("you") of the AI Email Helper Outlook add-in ("the Add-in").

Information We Collect

The Add-in itself does not collect or store any personal data on our servers. All email content remains within your Outlook environment. The Add-in provides optional integration with OpenAI or Azure OpenAI services through an API key that you provide. We do not store, transmit, or access your OpenAI credentials or your email data.

How the Information is Used

Your API key is stored locally in the browser (via Office.js storage) and is used solely to communicate with the OpenAI or Azure OpenAI API to generate summaries, replies, or other email content enhancements. No user data is transmitted to or stored by Blockchain Advisors Ltd.

This Add-in relies on third-party AI providers (OpenAI and Azure OpenAI). Please refer to their privacy policies for how your data is handled:
